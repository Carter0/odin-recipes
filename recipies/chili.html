Description

This deep and hearty stew will fill you up!

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Tomotoes
  • Beef Broth
  • Onion, Garlic, and pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Throw your beef into the pot and brown it
  2. Throw your spices into the pot and mix up the beef
  3. Throw in your chopped veggies
  4. Throw in tomatoes and beef broth
  5. Cook on low for 45 minutes till the flavors deepen
